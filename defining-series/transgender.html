Transgender

Definition

  • Transgender is an umbrella term for people whose gender identity, gender expression or behavior does not conform to that typically associated with the sex to which they were assigned at birth.
  • Transgender: People who do not identify with the gender assigned to them at birth
  • Gender Binary- people who identify with the opposite gender. Ex: Born a man, then identifying as a female
  • People who are not within the binary: Non binary, gender nonconforming gender fluid
  • *pronouns don't ALWAYS equal gender*
