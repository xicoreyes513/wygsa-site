Non-Binary

Definition

  • Non binary means existing outside of the gender binary
  • There are no established pronouns. Some people identify as being both a boy and a girl, or being neither a boy nor a girl
  • Most common pronouns: they/them
  • It is important to ask about pronouns rather than assuming pronouns

Breaking Down Non-Binary Flag

  • Yellow: Represents those whose gender exists outside
  • White: Represents people with many or all genders
  • Purple: Represents those whose gender identity falls somewhere between male/female or is a mix of them
  • Black: Represents people who do not identify with a gender
