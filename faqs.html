FAQ's

Question 1: What is GSA?

GSA is an acronym for Gay-Straight Alliance.

GSA is a student-led organization that unites LGBTQ+ and allied youth to form a group who educates topics regarding the LGBTQ community through posts and collabs. This organization creates a safe environment for all, where we not only stand up for our LGBTQ+ friends, but also other injustices within our community.

Question 2: What is our Mission?

Our ultimate goal in GSA is to to create a safe, welcoming, and accepting community for all youth, regardless of sexual orientation, gender identity, race and ethnicity. We also hope to educate others about the LGBTQ+ community from the meaning of flags to different types of sexual identifications.

Question 3:What does GSA do?

We create weekly posts such as defining LGBTQ terms, international LGBTQ days, the meaning behind flags, etc. We also collaborate with other inspirational accounts with goals similar to ours. We start each meeting with a check in where we can freely discuss how our week is going, and how we are feeling. We then begin discussing a proposed topic and create a post based on our conversation. We also participate in community pride events.

Question 4: Who can join Whittier Youth GSA?

Middle school and high school students can join the Whittier Youth GSA club (specifically 12-18 years olds).

Question 5: How do I join?

In order to join, please fill out our application! Once that is filled out, after 2-5 days, we will contact you with further information.

Question 6: Where do we meet and how often are meetings?

As of now, we meet every other Monday @ 6pm on Zoom. When you are accepted into the club, you will be contacted about the meeting information and added into our instagram groupchat

Question 7: Where can I contact Whittier Youth GSA?

Question 8: How many people are in Whittier Youth GSA?

There are currently 75 members in Whittier Youth GSA ranging from 6th grade to 12th grade. Come meet us!

Question 9: How long has this club been together?

Currently, we are going into our fourth year together. Whittier Youth GSA started as the Katherine Edwards Middle School GSA in 2019, and has since expanded into a community wide organization.

Question 10: What happens when I graduate high school?

When members graduate high school, they get taken out of the main groupchat and get placed in our WYGSA Alumni groupchat where you can still have a accepting / safe space but, also a space to make academic relationships with other WYGSA students out of high school.
